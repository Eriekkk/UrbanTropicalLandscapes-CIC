ABOUT US

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Urban Tropical Landscape is a team of passionate designers and artists from the Philippines, specializing in creating evolving landscapes that blend beauty and functionality. Established in 2007 and rebranded in the UAE in 2017, we bring over 17 years of expertise in crafting exclusive tropical landscapes, including Bali-inspired gardens, traditional Japanese garden styles, and modern custom designs.

With exceptional craftsmanship and innovative solutions, we transform urban spaces of all sizes into lush, breathtaking paradises. Create the perfect blend of nature and design with us!

OUR MISSION

At Urban Tropical Landscape, our mission is to provide top-tier landscaping services that seamlessly blend innovative design, expert construction, and meticulous maintenance.

We strive to create exceptional outdoor spaces that inspire and delight our clients, guaranteeing that every expense aligns with the superior quality of our services
